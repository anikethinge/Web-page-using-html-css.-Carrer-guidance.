────────────────────COMMERCE────────────────────

Commerce stream is chosen by students who have interest in subjects like Accountancy, Economics, Business Studies and Mathematics. After completing 12th in Commerce, students can pursue various undergraduate courses like B.Com, BBA, CA, CS and many more. Commerce stream is divided into two groups — With Maths which includes Accountancy, Economics, Mathematics and Business Studies, and Without Maths which includes Accountancy, Economics, Business Studies and Entrepreneurship. Commerce stream opens doors to careers in Banking, Finance, Management and Business fields.


Branches:

Commerce has two main branches. With Maths includes Accountancy, Economics, Mathematics and Business Studies leading to Banking and Finance. Without Maths includes Accountancy, Economics, Business Studies and Entrepreneurship leading to Management and Commerce fields.

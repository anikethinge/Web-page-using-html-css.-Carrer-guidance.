Career Guidance After 12th

What are the fields after 12th?

There are 3 main fields after 12th which are Science, Commerce, and Arts. Apart from these there are also Other vocational courses available. Most Indian colleges give admission on the basis of these streams.

Why do we need bifurcation?

Students study all subjects till class 10th. After that, each subject becomes very vast and detailed. So bifurcation is done so that students can focus on specific subjects and get in-depth knowledge of their chosen field.

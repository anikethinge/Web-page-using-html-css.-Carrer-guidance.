────────────────────ARTS────────────────────

Arts stream is chosen by students who have interest in subjects like History, Geography, Political Science, Psychology and Sociology. After completing 12th in Arts, students can pursue various undergraduate courses like BA, LLB, BJournalism, BFA and many more. Arts stream is one of the most diverse streams offering a wide range of career options in government, media, law and creative fields. Arts stream requires creativity and analytical thinking and leads to some of the most respected careers in India and abroad.


Branches:

Arts has many branches including History, Geography, Political Science, Psychology, Sociology, Philosophy and Fine Arts. It is one of the most diverse streams offering a wide range of career options.

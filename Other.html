────────────────────OTHERS────────────────────

Others stream includes all those fields which are outside the traditional Science Commerce and Arts streams. These include Vocational courses, Diploma programs, ITI trades, Fine Arts, Music, Sports, Fashion Design, Animation and many more skill based courses. These fields are perfect for students who have a specific passion or talent and want to build a career around it.


Branches:

Others stream covers Vocational courses, ITI trades, Diploma programs, Fine Arts, Music, Sports, Fashion Design, Animation and many more skill based courses outside the traditional three streams.

  • Vocational Courses
  • ITI Trades
  • Diploma Programs
  • Fine Arts and Design
  • Music and Performing Arts
  • Sports and Athletics
  • Fashion Design
  • Animation and Multimedia

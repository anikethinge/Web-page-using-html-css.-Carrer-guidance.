────────────────────SCIENCE────────────────────

Science stream is chosen by students who have interest in subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and Biology. After completing 12th in Science, students can pursue various undergraduate courses like B.Tech, MBBS, B.Sc, BCA and many more. Science stream is divided into two groups — PCM which includes Physics Chemistry and Maths, and PCB which includes Physics Chemistry and Biology. PCM students mostly go into Engineering, Architecture, Computer Science and Technology fields. PCB students mostly go into Medical, Pharmacy, Biotechnology and Nursing fields. Some students also take PCMB which includes all four subjects giving them more career options. Science stream requires dedication and hard work but it leads to some of the most respected careers in India and abroad.


Branches:

Science has two main branches. PCM includes Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics leading to Engineering and Technology. PCB includes Physics, Chemistry and Biology leading to Medical and Life Sciences
